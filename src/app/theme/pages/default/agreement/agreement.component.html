MEMBER RIGHTS AGREEMENT

This GENERAL RIGHTS AGREEMENT (the “Agreement”) spells out the rights and responsibilities of all parties named herein, being Originators, Sharers, Contractors (the “Members”), and The Black Box Digital Guild Inc. “Black Box” and together with the Members, the “Parties” or individual a Party), and as more specifically set out in Schedule “A” hereto, with respect to creation of digital media content (the “Work”), and any subsequent monetization of the Work.

WHEREAS Black Box is a Canada Corporation located in Ottawa, Canada;

AND WHEREAS Black Box has created a member only website (www.blackbox.global) (the “Platform”);

AND WHEREAS the Work is comprised of any and all digital media content that has been uploaded by a Contributor to the Platform (stock clips, a set of stock clips, a feature film, a short film, animations, or any other digital media content being created as part of a collaborative or non-collaborative efforts of a Member(s)) and uploaded to or processed by the Platform. 

NOW THEREFORE, for good and valuable consideration, the receipt and sufficiency of which is hereby acknowledged, the Parties hereby agree as follows:

  1. Definitions:

    2. Work.  The Work is a piece of intellectual property either completed or to be completed and that is the unique creation of Members.

    Copyright. Copyright shall have the legal meaning as defined by the Copyright Act of Canada and the Copyright Modernization Act, both as amended, and in the event material subject to copyright originates outside of Canada, as defined by the laws of the country of origin.

    Platform. The Platform is a private web site www.blackbox.global for Members. The Platform is owned and operated by The Black Box Digital Guild Inc., a Corporation registered in Canada.

    Fractional Ownership. Members may be offered a “share” in the Work, in proportion to the deemed fair market value of their contribution, as specified in various processes and functions of the Platform. This is a fractional ownership share of copyright, and entails all rights of copyright ownership including right to pro-rata share of net revenues and assignability

    Share. The percentage of copyright ownership assigned to and agreed to between Members.

    Sharer. Any Member who has been offered or designated a Share in the Work will be considered to be a Sharer in that Work.

    Member. A Member is an Entity that has registered and been accepted as a member of the Platform.

    Originator. The Originator is a Member that initiates a Project on the Platform. There are no restrictions on the percentage of copyright that an Originator will own of any given piece of Work.

    Project. Any collaborative use of the Platform will be considered as a Project

    Entity.  An Entity is a legally recognized individual or a legally constituted business - which could be a sole proprietorship, a business partnership, a corporation, or co-operative operating under the business rules of Canada, a Province, or a foreign jurisdiction.

    Contributor. Contributor means any Member providing money, goods, or services for incorporation in, or support for, the Work in exchange for a Share in the Work.

    Contractor.  Contractor means any Entity that provides goods or services in support of the production or marketing of the Work in exchange for a fixed payment for the goods and services.  A Contractor has no ongoing Share in the Work.

    Funder.  Funder means any Entity providing a financial contribution to the Work.  A Funder may have first call on proceeds derived from the marketing or selling of the Work, may have phased repayments, may have a Share, or may be otherwise compensated through recognition or product placement in accordance with the terms between the Funder and Member.

    Contribution. A Contribution is any part of the finished Work that is provided by a Contributor.  A Contribution may consist of intellectual property owned by the Contributor, or created by the Contributor expressly for the purpose of the Work.

    Storefront: Any sales or marketing agent or external delivery system is considered to be a Storefront.

    Net Revenue. Net Revenue means the gross proceeds of the Work [minus the Service Fee].

    Service Fee: Service Fee means a flat 15% fee charged by the Platform to Members calculated on the revenue net of non-Platform Storefront commissions.

  2. The Platform:
    1. The Platform is a private website for Members that facilitates:

      2. (a) A Members collaboration in the creation of the Work;

      (b) The processing of the Work and its distribution to Storefronts;

      (c) The collection of the Net Revenue from Storefronts; and

      (d) The distribution of Net Revenue to Members based on their Share in the Work.

    2. The Platform will deduct and retain as a Service Fee equal to fifteen (15%) percent of the gross revenue realized from the Storefronts, net of any third party service fees or expenses (ie. brokerage, marketing etc.).
    3. It is recognized and agreed by each Member having a Share in the Work that the title of the Work may be changed for marketing purposes or other good and proper reasons, and that any Share in the Work survives any such title change.
    4. By clicking accept to this Agreement as part of the registration to become a Member of the Platform, each Member agrees to be bound by this Agreement. This Agreement, as amended, shall be available for review at www.blackbox.global for review and each Member agrees and confirms that Black Box can alter the terms and conditions without notice to the Members.
    5. It is recognized and agreed that all terms and conditions as part of the Platform and Project operations must be fulfilled before a Members Share may be recognized.
    6. It is recognized and agreed that the timely performance of obligations under the terms of a collaborative Project is critical to successful completion of the Work.  Accordingly, the Platform reserves the right to intervene on behalf of Members in order to adjudicate any issues or disagreements or performance deficiencies by Members.
  3. Intellectual Property:
    1. Copyright in the Work shall at all times be held by an Entity who is a Party to this Agreement, and therefore is subject to all terms and conditions contained herein, and with Copyright with this General Rights Agreement and in accordance with Copyright law and generally accepted practice. 
    2. Nothing in this description of Copyright should be construed as preventing or attempting to prevent “fair use” of small portions of the Work or ancillary materials resulting from creation of the Work by any Member for personal, educational, or promotional purposes. 
  4. Transfer or Assignment of Copyright

    5. In the event that Copyright of a Work is to transferred or assigned in accordance with this Agreement, either during the development of the Work or following completion of the Work, the following conditions will apply:

    a) such transfer must have the unanimous support of all Share Members in the Work at the time of the proposed transfer;

    b) the transfer terms must commit the new Member holding the Copyright to the terms and conditions of this Agreement, which interests survive the sale or assignment of Copyright in the Work;

    c) the transfer terms must commit the new Member holding the Copyright to the terms of all Project commitments for outstanding Work that has commenced prior to the transfer date, but might not be completed as of the transfer date, or negotiate a fair settlement acceptable of all Members holding Shares in the Work.

  5. Management of Share Interest Distribution

    6. Net Revenue will flow through and be controlled by the Platform and will be distributed to the Members holding Shares in the Work in accordance with their percentage of ownership. The following conditions apply:

    a) Net Revenue will be reported to those Members holding a Share in the Work not less frequently than the end of each calendar month and payments will be made no later than the twentieth (20th) day of the following month; and

    b) the distribution of the Net Revenue to the Members holding Shares will be subject to reporting and withholding amounts in accordance with the tax rules of the country of distribution and/or international tax treaties.

  6. Confidentiality

      2. a) the information concerning a Party's past, present and future business affairs including, without limitation, finances, supplier information, services, products, organizational structure and internal practices, forecasts, sales and other financial results, records and budgets, and business, marketing, development, sales and other commercial strategies;

      b) creations, ideas, methods and discoveries, trade secrets, know-how, and other confidential intellectual property;

      c) any third-party confidential information included with, or incorporated into, any information provided by a Member or Black Box including, without limitation, any personal information; and

      d) other information that would reasonably be considered non-public, confidential or proprietary given the nature of the information and the Party’s business.

      3. a) is, or thereafter becomes, generally available to and known by the public other than as a result of its disclosure, directly or indirectly;

    4. Return of Confidential Information. Upon the expiration or termination of the Agreement, the receiving Party shall promptly return to the disclosing Party all of the disclosing Party's Confidential Information whether in written, electronic or other form or media.
  7. Non-competition

    8. The Members attest and confirm that during the creation of any Work in which they own a Share, and for a period of one (1) year after completion of the Work, they will not engage, directly or indirectly, in any production or creation of any materials or projects outside of the boundaries of the Platform that could reasonably be considered to be competitive in terms of theme and genre to the Work.

  8. No Assignment

    9. The Members may not assign any of its rights under this Agreement without the prior written consent of Black Box. Any assignment or delegation in violation of this Section shall be null and void.

  9. Term and Termination
    1. Term. The term of this Agreement (the “Term”) commences on date on which this Agreement is accepted by the Member and continues thereafter in perpetuity, until sooner terminated as provided in Section 9.2.
    2. Termination. Each Party, in its sole discretion, may terminate this Agreement:

      3. a) at any time, without cause

      b) immediately, with cause.

    3. Each Party shall be owed any Net Revenue or Service Fees, as applicable, up until the date of Termination.
    4. Should a Member wish to Terminate this Agreement in accordance with Section 9.2(a) and at the time of Termination owns Shares in any Work, the Members owning Shares in each Work shall have a first right of refusal to purchase its pro rata proportion of the Share. Should the Members of the Work decline its right to purchase its pro rata amount of the selling Member’s Share, it may be sold to a third (3rd) party, who must become a Member of the Platform before purchasing the Share.

These CFDR rules may be varied, modified or amended by written agreement of both parties.
