My Dashboard:

This overview of your account shows you the following information: last login time, monthly earnings graph, revenue to date, collaborative invitations, available opportunities, BlackBox notifications, and recently approved member content.

If one of your expertise roles is set to "Curator", then you can toggle off and on your availability to do curation work from "MY DASHBOARD".

You can also search through your footage or Projects from this page.

 

My Blackbox:

  • The “WORKSPACE” tab is where you will find your edited clips after you upload them via FTP.

    You may be either the Primary Creator (Clip Owner) of these edited clips or you may be the Curator who has edited these clips for another member.

    You can add "Sharers"{{ 'required_symbol' | translate }} in this tab during the process of adding metadata.

    Once submitted, the footage will be moved to the "SUBMITTED CONTENT" tab.

    {{ 'required_symbol' | translate }}"Sharers" are BlackBox members who have contributed to the content creation in some way for a percentage of footage ownership and sales.

  • The "CURATION" tab will only appear if you have one of your expertise roles set to "Curator".

    From this tab, Metadata-Only Curators will be able to see edited clips assigned to them by other members. You have the option to add metadata{{ 'required_symbol' | translate }} using the "Edit" button or to decline clips using the "Refuse" button. You can also filter footage by owner.

    {{ 'required_symbol' | translate }}Metadata consists of a unique clip description, keywords, a category, any available model and/or property releases, editorial information when applicable, and optional notes.

  • The “SUBMITTED CONTENT” tab is where you will find a comprehensive list of all your submitted content.
    You can filter by "My Assets" for clips you have uploaded yourself and "My Shared Assets" for clips where you were a Curator or a Sharer. You can browse through the list or search for specific content using the search bar at the top right.
    In this tab, you can view content, approve work added by a Curator as part of a Project, and delete submitted content that has been rejected or still has "Pending Review" status.

    Submitted clips can display the following statuses:
    “Pending Review” - the footage is waiting for the BlackBox reviewer to approve.
    “Ready” - the footage has been submitted to BlackBox, was reviewed/approved, and is ready for delivery to our partner agencies.
    “Online” - the footage has been successfully accepted by at least one of our partner agencies.
    “Pending” - the footage is waiting for the Clip Owner’s approval after a Curator finished their work.
    “Rejected” - the footage was rejected by all of our partner agencies due to quality issues or redundancy. The footage can be deleted at this stage.

  • The “MY COLLABORATION” tab is for creating new Projects and working on Projects in progress.
    In this tab, you can create, save, submit, and complete Projects. You can upload and download Project content from this tab as either the Project Owner or the Curator and use the chat feature to share details and ask questions.

  • The “MARKETPLACE” tab is the area where you will see postings of all available Projects. Here you can browse Projects and accept a Project if you are a Curator. You can use the search filter to see either "All" or "My Postings"

 

My Earnings:

  • The “My Earnings” tab gives you access to your revenue earnings report.

  • “My Revenue” lists the earnings of recent sales that have not yet been paid out.

  • “Total Earnings Report” lists all sold assets from a selected time period.

  • “Payment History” lists all monthly payouts which have been paid to you.

  • "Annual Summary" provides a revenue report for your own accounting or tax purposes.

 

To upload content:

Go to “My Backbox” menu

  • After you upload your clips via FTP{{ 'required_symbol' | translate }}, your content will be transferred to the "WORKSPACE" tab.

    The current supported FTP programs are FileZilla, WinSCP and Totalcommander.

    {{ 'required_symbol' | translate }}Check out the User Guide for complete FTP instructions.

 

To upload Metadata: ("Metadata" in this case refers to a unique clip description, keywords, a category, any available model and/or property releases, and editorial information when applicable.)

You can add Metadata manually by using the “Edit” window
- or -
You can prepare the Metadata in a CSV/XLS file outside of Blackbox. When you upload the finished document, your metadata will be added to your corresponding uploaded clips.

CSV/XLS Instructions:
  • You can download the .XLS template with the required format here
  • Fill out all required fields.
  • Click on the “Upload CSV/XLS” button.
    NOTE: The "Upload CSV/XLS" button is only visible if you have uploaded file(s) in your WORKSPACE or CURATION tab.
  • Select your completed .XLS file to upload.
  • The data in the .XLS file will automatically be added to the matching clips in your “WORKSPACE” tab.
  • If you create a batch for a group of clips, you can then select the “apply to batch”{{ 'required_symbol' | translate }} option for selected field(s), which will copy that same metadata to all of the files under that batch name.

{{ 'required_symbol' | translate }}"Apply to batch" will take the metadata field you select (description, keywords, category, model release, property release, or a combo of any of these), and apply them to all the clips you have grouped together under the same batch name.

 

To upload Project Files:

  • The following process is for Curators working on content provided by another member as part of a Project when edited clips are ready to be uploaded.
  • If you are working on a current Curation Project, a button will appear in your "WORKSPACE" tab that says, "Upload Project Files". This button will not be visible to you if you are not currently working on a Collaborative Project.
    Clicking on the "Upload Project Files" button will send you to the project page in the "MY COLLABORATION" tab. From that project page, you can upload your final edited clips.
  • Once the clips are uploaded, you will find them in the "WORKSPACE" tab where you can add metadata and submit the clips.

 

To share content ownership and revenue:

You can add Sharers and assign them an ownership percentage of the content within the metadata "Edit" section of the "WORKPLACE" tab.

For a collaborative project, you can specify the Curator and Sharers’ ownership percentages in the "Create a Project" window of the "MY COLLABORATION" tab when setting up a new project.

BlackBox will then automatically distribute the submitted content to global markets (i.e. our partner agencies). Every time shared content sells, BlackBox will automatically split the revenue among all sharers involved.

 

To create a collaborative curation project:

In the “MY COLLABORATION” tab, create a new project and select the project type "Curation Project". In the following window, you can select your collaborators (Curator and Sharers).

NOTE: At this time, you can only select one Curator, but can add multiple Sharer(s).

You can pick your own Curator or you can post the project to the "MARKETPLACE" where qualified Curators can request to collaborate on your project.

The Curator can submit edited footage on your behalf, which you need to approve before submitting the project for distribution to the agencies.

 

Curation process:

  • When working with a Curator, you can ask for Partial or Full Curation.

    - Partial curation – Editing and Uploading Only or Metadata Only
    You can ask an Editor to edit and upload only, or you can ask a Metadata Curator to do your metadata tagging only (description, keywords, category, editorial information when applicable).

    For Editing and Uploading Only, you can choose to specify the type of work needed (such as Export, Grading, Selects, and Other Processes), and can also write a note to your Curator with additional information.

    For Metadata Only, you can specify whether you will transfer the content to the Curator outside of BlackBox.
    - or -
    (Recommended) You can specify that you will upload the edited content yourself and assign the clips to your agreed Curator from your "WORKSPACE" tab.

    You can create a project to do partial curation for Editing and Uploading in collaboration with one Curator, then open a second project do metadata tagging in collaboration with another Curator separately.

    - Full curation – Editing, Uploading, and Metadata
    In this case, you will arrange to transfer your content to your Curator outside of the BlackBox platform and they will finish the entire curation process.

  • When creating a new project, you can set the project as "Private" or as "Marketplace".
    If your project is set as "Private", then only the Curator you have selected will be able to see your project in their "MARKETPLACE" tab. An email will be sent to that curator asking them to accept or reject the project.

  • If your project is set as "Marketplace", then any member can express an interest in curating your project and you can approve the Curator you prefer.

  • When creating a new project, the "Save" button will save the unpublished project to your "MY COLLABORATION" tab and will not post it to the "MARKETPLACE" yet.
    "Submit" will submit your project to the "MARKETPLACE" tab.

  • Interested Collaborators and Curators can chat with the Project Owner to ask for more information about the project and the work involved.
    After the Project Owner assigns a Curator, that Curator can then accept or reject the project assignment.

  • Once the Curator finishes the curation work, they can then upload the finished clips on behalf of the Project Owner for approval.
    In the "MY COLLABORATION" tab, the Curator can use the “Upload project files” button within the project to upload the finished footage. The project name becomes the "batch name" for the Curator’s organizational purposes.
    Uploaded footage will become visible in the "WORKSPACE" tab for the final steps of metadata curation before finally submitting the clips.

  • After submitting the footage on the Project Owner's behalf, the Curator can mark the Project as “complete”. The Project owner will get an email notification so they can either approve the footage or simply mark the Project “complete”.
    NOTE: If the Project Owner marks the Project as “complete” before approving the individual clips, all the footage within that Project will be automatically approved.
